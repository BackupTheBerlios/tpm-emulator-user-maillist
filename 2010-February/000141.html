Dear all,

I am happy to announce the latest release of the TPM Emulator
(see https://developer.berlios.de/project/showfiles.php?group_id=2491).

Starting with this release, the emulator runs not only on Linux, but
also on Mac OS X and Windows. In addition, the emulator now
also supports MTM emulation. Many thanks to all those who
contributed to his achievement; in particular to Amit Singh, Domenic
Schroeder, Jan-Erik Ekberg, and Markku Kylanpaa.

As usual, I kindly ask you to test this release with all  tools and test
cases available to you and to report any bugs or compatibility issues
to me. I further welcome any information on (un)successful attempts
to run/port the emulator to other platforms than the above mentioned,
in particular FreeBSD and OpenBSD. Thank you very much for your help!

Kind regards,
Mario
