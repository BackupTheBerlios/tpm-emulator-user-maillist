On 11/8/11 11:07 PM, Blumenthal, Uri - 0668 - MITLL wrote:
> Can you please tell me what file(s) of the CMAKE config to
> edit and what lines to add there to tell CMAKE that:
> 
> - Related include files are in /opt/local/include
> - Related libraries are in /opt/local/lib
You can specify them with

include_directories("/opt/local/include")
link_directories("/opt/local/lib")

in the main CMakeLists.txt file -- or simply update the
generated Makefile in build.
