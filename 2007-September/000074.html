On Mon, Sep 10, 2007 at 02:22:53PM +0200, sschuetz at fhm.edu wrote:

> TCS tcspbg.c:221 Entering OSAP
> To TPM: 00 C1 00 00 00 24 00 00 00 0B 00 01 40 00 00 00

That means, a OSAP session is requested with entity type
TPM_ET_KEYHANDLE and entity value TPM_KH_SRK. According to the
TPM specification v1.2 rev 103 such a request is allowed and
should be handled by the emulator.
